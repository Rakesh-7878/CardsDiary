Frequently Asked Questions

How do I customize my digital invitation?

It's incredibly simple! Choose your favorite template from our collection, click 'Customize', and personalize your details, colors, and fonts directly in our intuitive drag-and-drop editor.

When will I receive my digital card?

Instantly! The moment you finalize your design, you can download it immediately in high resolution. Share it instantly via WhatsApp, Email, or any Social Media platform.

Are there any hidden costs or printing fees?

None at all. Our digital invitations are a sustainable, cost-effective alternative to traditional paper cards. You pay once for the premium design, with no printing or shipping fees.

Is my invitation suitable for mobile viewing?

Absolutely. Every invitation is meticulously crafted to be fully responsive. They look flawlessly elegant on any smartphone, tablet, or desktop screen.

Can I use my own photos in the design?

Yes, many of our premium templates allow you to upload and seamlessly integrate your own beautiful photos, making your invitation truly one-of-a-kind and personal.
