Privacy Policy

At CardsDiary, we deeply respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

  • The website may collect basic analytics information such as page views, device type, and browser information to help us improve the user experience.
  • Cookies may be used to improve website performance, remember your preferences, and enhance overall functionality.

How We Use Your Information

  • To understand how visitors use our platform and to improve our services.
  • To enhance website performance and ensure smooth functionality.
  • To communicate important updates about CardsDiary when you contact us.

Data Protection

  • User invitation data is not sold to third parties under any circumstances.

  • We do not share your data with external organizations for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

CardsDiary may use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to understand website traffic patterns. These services may collect anonymized usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
